About me

September 28, 2025

—

Pierina from Peru

Hello! My name is Pierina, and I’m from Peru. I created this blog as a space to share a bit about myself, my journey, and the things I love most in life — nature, teaching, and connecting with others.

My Background

When I was younger, I worked in the fashion industry as a model. At first, I enjoyed it, but over time, I began to feel disconnected. It started to feel too superficial. I realized that I admire the natural beauty of a woman much more than the artificial standards often pushed by the media — all the makeup, surgeries, and pressure to look a certain way.

Thanks to my experience in that field, I later started my own small business offering eyelash services — with a more natural and personal touch.

A Dream to Teach

As a child, I always dreamed of becoming a teacher. However, since my family worked in agriculture, I felt pressure to study agro-food engineering instead. I was close to finishing my degree when I went through a very difficult time: a divorce that left me feeling lost and betrayed.

That painful experience pushed me to make a bold decision — I moved to the Amazon region of Peru to start over and heal.

Healing in the Heart of Nature

Living in the Amazonas changed my life. I reconnected with nature, and with myself. The peace, the beauty of the jungle, the wild animals, the kind and welcoming people — everything felt so pure. It was far away from the noise and stress of the city, and exactly what I needed to recover.

Amasonas Region

While I was there, I started teaching basic English in a local school. I loved it. It made me realize how much I enjoy helping children learn. Each student is different, and I enjoy the challenge of adapting and finding the best way to help them succeed.

From Engineering to Education

After nearly a year in Amazonas, I returned to Chiclayo to finish my degree — but life had other plans for me. I was offered a new opportunity to teach at a school, and I followed my heart. I left the engineering path and fully embraced education.

Now, I teach English to primary and secondary students. I prepare exams, create homework, and keep in touch with parents to support each student’s progress. I’ve also started teaching Spanish privately — something I love because it allows me to share my language and culture with people from all over the world.

My Mission

Teaching gives me a sense of purpose. I want to help as many people as I can — whether they are children learning their first English words, or adults discovering Spanish for the first time. Sharing knowledge, connecting with others, and growing together — that’s what truly makes me happy.

Thank you for reading my story. I’m excited to keep sharing my journey with you through this blog.

With love, Pierina
